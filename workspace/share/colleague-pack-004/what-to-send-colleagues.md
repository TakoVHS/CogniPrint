## Suggested framing

Send these materials as a compact colleague-review package for a follow-up manuscript. The correct description is:

`CogniPrint currently has a working empirical evidence package supporting a follow-up manuscript. The current repository state includes 5 controlled perturbation campaigns and 41 comparison rows, with provenance-linked inputs, campaign summaries, dataset scaffolds, and manuscript drafting outputs.`

## What not to claim

- Do not describe the package as a finished study.
- Do not claim conclusive support or broad generalization.
- Do not describe the outputs as source or identity attribution.
- Do not describe the workflow as a forensic or legal instrument.

## Review question to ask colleagues

Ask for feedback on whether the current Methods, Results, and Limitations language is appropriately bounded for the present evidence base, and whether the evidence table is sufficient for manuscript orientation.
