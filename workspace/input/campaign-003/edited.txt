CogniPrint is being developed as a reproducible research workstation for examining compact statistical text profiles. In the current workflow, one baseline note is compared with controlled variants that keep the same topic while altering only one main dimension at a time. The objective is not to make final claims about authorship or source. Instead, the system records profile shifts, metric changes, and campaign summaries that can be reviewed across repeated perturbation studies. This allows scientific colleagues to discuss stability tendencies by referring to explicit artifacts instead of relying only on intuition.
