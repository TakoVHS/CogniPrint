CogniPrint is being developed as a reproducible research workstation for studying compact statistical profiles of text; in the current workflow, a baseline note is paired with controlled variants that preserve the topic while changing one main dimension at a time. The goal is not to produce definitive judgments about authorship or source. Instead, the system records profile shifts, metric deltas, and campaign-level summaries that can be compared across repeated perturbation studies. This makes it possible to discuss stability tendencies with scientific colleagues, using explicit artifacts rather than intuition alone.
