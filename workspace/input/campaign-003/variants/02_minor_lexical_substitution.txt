CogniPrint is being developed as a reproducible research workstation for studying compact statistical representations of text. In the current workflow, a baseline note is matched with controlled variants that preserve the topic while modifying one principal dimension at a time. The aim is not to produce final judgments about text origin. Instead, the system records profile shifts, metric differences, and campaign-level summaries that may be compared across repeated perturbation studies. This makes it possible to discuss stability tendencies with scientific colleagues by using explicit artifacts rather than intuition alone.
