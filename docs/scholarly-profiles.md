## Medium profile

Adriashkin Roman is an independent researcher and founder of the CogniPrint Research Initiative. His current work studies compact statistical profiles of text, termed cognitive fingerprints, with emphasis on feature-space geometry, similarity structure, and perturbation stability. CogniPrint is developed as an open research programme with a manuscript layer, citation metadata, and reproducible software assets.
