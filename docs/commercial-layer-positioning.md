# Commercial Layer Positioning

CogniPrint remains a research-first project. The mathematical core, empirical workstation, evidence snapshot, benchmark layer, and validation materials remain separate from any optional hosted billing surface.

## Boundary

- The research core stays open and research-oriented.
- The billing layer is optional and bounded.
- Paid access may cover hosted convenience, API access, managed reports, private workspaces, support, or managed analysis.
- Paid access does not imply stronger scientific claims.
- Paid access does not turn outputs into final judgments.
- The public evidence snapshot remains public and separate.
- CogniPrint is not a forensic or attribution decision system.

## Pricing Concept

- `Starter`
  - individual researchers
  - monthly subscription
  - hosted analysis convenience
  - limited usage
- `Research Pro`
  - labs and independent research teams
  - higher usage
  - report exports
  - private project workspace
- `Institution`
  - manual contact
  - review-oriented support
  - audit-friendly workflows
