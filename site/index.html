Research Focus

CogniPrint investigates the mathematical structure of text through compact statistical profiles, termed cognitive fingerprints. The work focuses on feature-space geometry, profile similarity, and stability of empirical regularities under controlled perturbations.

The goal is to develop a reproducible mathematical basis for quantitative text analysis, not to provide definitive source judgments.
