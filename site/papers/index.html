Cognitive Fingerprints: A Mathematical Framework for Statistical Profiling of Text

Author: Roman Adriashkin
Date: 2026 (v0.1)

Abstract: We introduce a mathematical framework for representing text samples by compact finite-dimensional profiles, termed cognitive fingerprints. The framework is based on a feature map from token sequences into a Euclidean space of empirical statistics. This representation supports quantitative comparison of text samples through profile distances and similarity measures, as well as perturbation-based questions about stability under bounded edits.
